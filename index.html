About Me

I'm very passionate about tech startups, and have co-founded and helped grow a couple over the past decade (typically in a business role, as CEO or head of business development and strategy). As a Harvard MBA and "business guy", everyone always told me "you don't need to know how to code". I ignored them all because the ability to understand technology, build tangible products, and make improvements to our solutions is very rewarding to me...even as the business co-founder. Excel and Powerpoint are nowhere near as cool as javascript! Outside of work, I love to surf, play guitar, sing, play soccer, travel, and eat Peruvian food! This is a sample of my work thus-far, which I expect to continuously update as I keep learning how to build cool things. Thanks for visiting my portfolio page!
